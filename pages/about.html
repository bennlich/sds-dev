About

As SUPERDUPERSTUDIO, Matt and Chris appropriate non-design methodologies, and design projects that result in unforeseeable outcomes. By experimenting with procedures, processes, appropriation, collage, and other systems for making things, we create works that are at once singular and accessible.

In addition to technical expertise, we are motivated by cultural theory, systems, and the scientific method.

We generate our own projects and products, and like to collaborate with clients, artists, and other designers, in addition to scientists, jugglers, musicians, writers, programmers, progressive snake charmers, and all other sorts of creative types.

We believe in making things that are mysterious yet communicative; inviting but challenging; elegant and complex. We are most interested in projects that are discursive by nature, so let’s talk!

Call or email anytime with ideas for collaboration (we're currently in San Francisco), or just to say “Hey SUPERDUPERSTUDIO! Hey!”
